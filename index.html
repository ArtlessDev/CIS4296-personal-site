OwlHacks 2023

This application was created in 36 hours. It's use case is for in-person classrooms. How it works is, with a database, setup with Firebase, a teacher will be able to login to their classroom and provide a generated classroom code to their students. For children, they may not have the courage to verbally ask a question out of fear of being mocked; this allows those students to ask the questions that they have just like their classmates.
